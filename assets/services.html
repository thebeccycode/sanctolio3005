Services

Our business offers digital services from website building to running your social media business pages. Below are the services and the prices you can expect to pay for them:


Logo Design

£100

We will work with you to design and create a logo that is perfect for your company. Whether you already know what you want, or you don't, we will make sure that you are proud to display your brand new logo that you can trademark.


Website Design

£50

Although this is part of the web development process, it is necessary that this is a separate service to website development. if you already have your own wireframe, you will not need to pay this. It is a relatively quick process once you have picked your 'Elements' from our Client Guide, but if you recieve the prototype and are not happy with it, we can tweak it at no extra cost. A complete re-do will incur another £50 charge.


Website Development

£500 - £1000 ◆ 30% deposit required

This price is inclusive of hosting and domain name fees and depends on the size of the website you require. This process can take between 4-12 weeks depending on the size of the website you require. You will be able to see previews of your website as it reaches different milestones so that you can make any design changes as you go along.


Social Media Marketing

In order to post, you will need to provide content and updates which we will ask for once a week. You decide how much you want us to post and interact.

Brand Packages ⪢ £150

This includes a logo design, post templates, post elements and colour palette creation.

Social Media Set Up ⪢ £80

This is for the time it takes to set up the social media platforms and promote them.

Multi-Platform Post ⪢ £25 per post

This means that 1 post is shared across all platforms e.g. Facebook, Instagram, Reels, TikTok etc.

Social Media Admin ⪢ £30 per week

This means that I will be monitoring and replying to your customers in a timely manner and making sure to signpost them back to you. This is optional, but some clients find this service helps them free up their own time.


Payment Plans

We want to help you out if you're a new business and you don't want to fork out so much money at once. Therefore we offer payment plans with website design and development. This is something to discuss at the initial appointment.
